Ever had chicken tandoori with Angrezi Twist ?? Come at ANGREZI DHABA and enjoy the various angrezi twists here!!!



Price for two:  1,100

  022 30151010

  2nd Floor, Nakshtra Mall, Dadar West, Mumbai

  19-22, Shree Ambika Heritage, Plot 1, Sector 1, Kharghar, Navi Mumbai

  Todi Mill Compound, Lower Parel, Mumbai


Great For:

Delicious Indian Food with an Angrezi Twist Filmy Ambience A lot of variety to choose from Distinct and very filmy way to present the dishes

The name itself gives a distinctive feeling and the food and the interior was certainly quote unique!!

What you must Try ?

  • Chicken popcorn

    • These were different from the ones we have it as KFC and certainly quite delicious. The crispy salty cover on the outside,moist, tender soft chicken on the inside tasted phenomenal with mayo sauce!

  • Fish fingers

    • The American concept but with Indian taste!! The crispy and masala type fish fingers were absolutely amazing.Again they tasted spectacular with mayo sauce and tomato sauce.Have tasted better this is certainly an good thing to taste here

  • Angrezi mantra

    • Made with green apple and pineapple juice. The sweet and sour combination was definitely welcoming..

  • Fruit Punch

    • Juicy ,fresh fruit punch which can be readily enjoyed with delight

  • Chocolate overload:

    • For all the chocolate lovers out there ,the ultimate chocolate dish. There were load of dark chocolate,milk chocolate and some chocolate with brownies nuts and cream all mixed into one and topped with some gems .It might not look much appetizing but it certainly tasted incredible..

What Could be Improved ?

  • Paneer chilli cheese kebab

    • These weren't that up to the mark . The paneer was soft and cheese tasted okay but they need add more flavours and seasoning to it.Could be a lot better.

    Comments on Ambience and Service?

    Ambience is quite bright and colorful . You will enjoy eating heres
