Healthy+Tasty ??Check LOCO LOCA for lip-smacking Spanish-Indian Fusion



Price for two:  1,200

  +91 9137528772

  The Empresa Hotel, Sab TV Lane, Andheri Lokhandwala, Andheri West, Mumbai


Great For:

Spanish-Indian Fusion Open Roof Ambience Unique dishes such as Chocalate Cigar

his place is definitely one of the best places who would be known for their great food in the future.The experience was incredible and flawless. The food cooked to perfection and the ambience , peaceful and vibrant..

What you must Try ?

  • FETA CHEESE AND CO.

    • This salad changed my view on healthy food. The vegetables were soft and yet all the nutrients and taste were still intact. The vegetables are seasoned ,wrapped in foil and cooked to perfection in charcoal.The technique is quite elaborate and it reflects in the taste of this salad

  • FUNGI POPPERS:

    • These Mushrooms poppers had three types of mushrooms in it,namely Portobello,button mushrooms and cili mushrooms.They also served it with a unusual dip which tasted marvellous with the poppers.

  • Portobello mushrooms and grilled asparagus rissoto

    • Ever had a rissoto before ?This one was perfect, delicious and healthy.Even in the pics you can see how lovely and vibrant the dish looks.The texture was perfect. It consisted of perfectly cooked Risotto with ample of mushrooms ,grilled asparagus and some seasoning.HEALTHY AND A DELICIOUS DISH.

  • CHOCOLATE WHISKY AND SMOKE CIGAR ROLL

    • This dessert was definitely new to me. All the cigar and whiskey lovers must try this once. I could taste the chocolate, whiskey and at the end the smokey taste.The presentation was done beautifully too.HIGHLY RECOMMENDED.

What Could be Improved ?

Nothing from the dishes I tried

Comments on Ambience and Service?

Open roof cafe and bright ambience.
