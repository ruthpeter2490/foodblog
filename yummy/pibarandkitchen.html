Check Out Pi Pizzeria & Pi Bar and Kitchen for some delicious appetizers and pizzas!!



Price for two:  1,300

  022 26359494
 022 26390044

  B-12, Ghanshyam Chambers, Link Road, Opposite Citi Mall, Andheri Lokhandwala, Andheri West

  Shop 7, Darvesh Grand, Next to Bharat Bank, 4th Road, Khar, Mumbai


Great For:

Italian Food Peaceful Ambience AWESOME TIRAMASU

Pi Pizzeria and Pi Bar & Kitchen are different outlet offering the some food in some variations .Food is good but costly for the quality so upto customers now to what they prefer.Personally find the food a bit expensive.

What you must Try ?

  • Crunchy calamari

    • Crunchy calamari is squid coated with batter and deep fried. It tasted really great with mayo . The crunchiness and the softness of the squid were in perfect balance.

  • Harrisa Cottge Cheese

    • Harissa paneer is soft , tender pieces of paneer cooked in spicy red sauce. Loved the look on it when it arrived and with each bite the flavours with bursting in my mouth.

  • Pollo arrosoto

    • Pollo arrosoto had grilled chicken ,roasted peppers combined with pesto sauce and onions. Tasted incredibly . Highly recommended.

  • Tiramasu

    • This coffee dessert will capture your heart with its taste . It was that yummy. The light fluffy texture of the sponge ,the perfect creaminess between them was such a delight to the eyes and the tastebuds . HIGHLY RECOMMENDED

  • Baked Mushrooms in pesto sauce mozarella an olive oil

    • I would definitely recommend this for vegetarians . Consisted of mushrooms stuffed wih mozeerlla and seasoning

What Could be Improved ?

  • Garlic dough balls

    • Garlic dough balls with garlic butter need a lot of improvement since it's just plain bread with butter on top .I wouldn't recommend it ,needs heavy improvement.

  • Quattari

    • Quattari is a 4 cheese pizza. It had parmesan , fontal ,feta and mozzarella cheese. It was way too salty to taste.Cheeses didnt balance that well .

Comments on Ambience and Service?

Ambience is posh got to say.
