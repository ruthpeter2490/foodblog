Love Fusion Cuisine dishes ? Check Out this Wonderful Cafe At Juhu Called EDIBLE CRAFT



Price for two:  345

  022 33126272

  A-9, Kailash Apartments, Near Juhu Bus Depot, Juhu, Mumbai

Great For:

Fusion cuisine dishes Peaceful Ambience Waffles

A small quaint vegetarian cafe at Juhu , specialising in fusion concept .Every dish here is a fusion of some cuisines.

What you must Try ?

  • Makhni Pasta

    • Just loved the aroma when it arrived . Definetely captured the Italian and Indian essence in this dish . The perfect creaminess and the balance of flavours were something to die for!!

  • Lebanese Sandwich

    • Trying to bring the Lebanese cuisine here is quite commendable. The filling of the Sandwich consists of thinely sliced purple cabbage,carrots, onions ,mayo ,and burger patty which I couldn't identify what it was made of.Overall verdict it's nice to try with their dip.

  • Burmese Maggi

    • Ever want to have a fusion of Maggi and Burmese cuisine,this is definitely the place to try this dish.The flavours were so great ,just bursting in my mouth ,the aroma was blissful .This is best served and had while the dish the hot .

  • Jalebi and Rabdi waffles

    • Lovely concept of fusion of jalebis and waffles.Loved having it together with the jalebis .

  • Gulab Jamun waffles

    • When these arrived ,they looked so pretty!! This Fusion dish worked out so well. Enough for two people to devour and one of my favourite desserts.

    What Could be Improved ?

    • Masala Frappe

      • This fusion didnt quite reach its mark . He fusion of hot and cold went a miss and leads to you a luke warm coffee which could be best enjoyed hot.

    • Pizza Sev puri

      • Pizza on top of Puris taste wise its good but you end up not being satisfied due to the quantity .

    Comments on Ambience and Service?

    Its a small cafe but a very peaceful one and bright one. Service was good and really enjoyed the dishes here !!
